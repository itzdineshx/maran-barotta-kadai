Our Story

Maran Barotta Kadai started as a small family business in the heart of Tamil Nadu. What began as a passion for creating the perfect parotta has grown into one of the most beloved parotta destinations in the region.

We take pride in our traditional methods while constantly innovating to bring you new and exciting flavors. Every parotta is made with love and served with pride.
